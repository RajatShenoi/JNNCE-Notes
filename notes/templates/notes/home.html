Welcome!

Learn Easy is a learning resource sharing platform designed for students pursuing engineering at any of the colleges affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University. Resources are shared by students and are available for free.

Find resources Contribute

What's your benefit?

Whether it is your semester exams, internal assessments, assignment solutions, this community has got you covered. We have a wide range of resources available for you to access and download to ace your exams.

How does this work?

Unlike other platforms where the website owner uploads their resources, the resources here are shared by students for the benefit of the same and future batches.

How can I help?

By uploading notes sent by your professors or the ones you made, sharing your assignment solutions, important questions or by uploading your internal assessment and sem exam question papers as soon as it's completed.

The GOAL

The problem: Different professors share resources in different places (WA groups, Google Classroom, etc.) As a student, it's hard to manage.

The goal: of this platform is to create a one stop destination for any resources that you need so that you don't have to go through the hassle of searching for them. All this, by the community and not just one person.
